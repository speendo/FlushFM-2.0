# US-0009: Audio Buffer Fill Optimization

> **Status:** To Do  
> **Priority:** Medium  
> **Created:** 2026-03-28

---

## User Story

As a **user**,  
I want **audio quality to be consistent from the start of playback on all bitrates**,  
so that **the first ~1 second of audio doesn't sound degraded (especially on 192 kbps streams) while the buffer fills**.

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] 192 kbps streams no longer exhibit degraded audio quality during initial ~1 second playback
- [ ] Audio quality is consistent regardless of bitrate (128, 192, 256 kbps tested)
- [ ] Playback starts immediately when `play` command is issued (no artificial delay to wait for full buffer)
- [ ] Root cause identified and documented (decoder sync issue, buffer underrun, or sample rate mismatch)
- [ ] Audio remains stable throughout playback (no stuttering, dropouts, or quality degradation)
- [ ] Solution does not cause ESP32 memory exhaustion or crashes (heap usage stays reasonable; device remains stable under extended playback)
- [ ] Solution tested on 128, 192, and 256 kbps streams with varying network latency

---

## Investigation Notes

- **Observation:** 192 kbps streams show poor audio quality for approximately 1 second after `play` command, even though data is arriving
- **Reproduced With:** 
  - https://orf-live.ors-shoutcast.at/fm4-q2a (ORF FM4 station, 192 kbps MP3) — exhibits crackle/glitching at start
  - https://secureonair.krone.at/kronehit-ultra-hd.aac (Krone Hit Ultra HD, AAC format) — **no crackle observed**
- **Format Observation:** AAC streams start cleanly; MP3 streams exhibit quality degradation → issue may be codec-specific or bitrate-dependent
- **Characteristic:** Degradation sounds like underrun/glitching, not simply quiet audio (suggests buffer is being read but data is incomplete/corrupted)
- **Likely Root Causes:**
  1. **Decoder Sync Issue:** ESP32-audioI2S decoder may not properly synchronize with dynamic bitrate changes at startup
  2. **Buffer Underrun:** Audio task starts reading before enough valid data is buffered (happens faster than network data arrives)
  3. **Sample Rate Detection:** Decoder misdetects initial sample rate, causing resampling artifacts
- **Library:** `ESP32-audioI2S` (version 3.4.4 pinned in `platformio.ini`)
- **Key Constraint:** Solution must NOT introduce artificial delays → playback starts immediately, quality improves organically as buffer stabilizes
- **Testing:** Reproduce on 128, 192, 256 kbps with slow/fast networks to isolate root cause
